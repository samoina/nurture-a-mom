Maternal morbidity and mortality in Kenya results from the interplay of social, cultural, economic and logistical barriers, coupled with a high fertility rate and inadequate and under-funded health services. Strengthening the health system and improving quality of healthcare delivery is pivotal to reversing the trend of high maternal morbidity and mortality. Source

Nurture A mom provides innovative quality care to mothers and children through the first 1000 days of life - from pregnancy to postpartum, breastfeeding and weaning in response to the high maternal morbidity.

We do this by:

Increasing access to maternal and child healthcare services

Empowering women to be in charge of their pregnancies and take the guesswork out of the perinatal period

Providing peace of mind for women and families during and after pregnancy.

Advocate and partner with stakeholders to improve clinical care of women during the perinatal period.
