Personalized Packages

Birth Preparation Package

The birth preparation classes emlighten you and prepare you to be in charge during your birth experience.

Baby Care

A caregiver visits 48-hours after discharge from a health facility to help ensure that your baby is latching well during breastfeeding.

Postpartum Care

We offer the recovering mother and new born care and support that is tailored to their specific needs.

Mental health assessment

To deal with or avert stress-related problems during or after delivery, we conduct a mental health assessment of the mother after birth and make referrals for severe cases.

Breastfeeding support

Babies will seek the breast when they get skin-to-skin contact with their mother which we encourage during Kangaroo Mother Care.

Complimentary Feeding Support

Our caregivers train mothers on ideal meal prep, appropriate nutrition and portion sizes as the baby grows.
